How to Play

🃏

The Goal

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting).

🔢

Card Values

Number cards = face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10. Ace = 1 or 11 — whichever is better for your hand.

🎯

Your Options

  • Hit – draw another card
  • Stand – keep your current hand
  • Double Down – double your bet and receive exactly one more card
🏦

Dealer Rules

The dealer must hit on 16 or less and stand on 17 or more. One dealer card starts face-down.

💰

Payouts

  • Win → 1:1
  • Push (tie) → Bet back
  • Blackjack → 3:2
♠

Blackjack!

An Ace + a 10-value card on the first two cards = Blackjack. Pays 3:2 unless the dealer also has Blackjack (push).
